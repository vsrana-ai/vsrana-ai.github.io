Dr. Vivek Kumar (SMIEEE, Fellow-SCRS) is an NLP enthusiast and currently a Research Scientist at the [ADAPT Research Centre](https://www.adaptcentre.ie) at Trinity College Dublin,  University of Dublin, Ireland. Before this, he was a senior researcher at the University of Federal Armed Forces [(UniBwM)](https://www.unibw.de/aiml-en/persons/vivek-kumar-ph-d) under the Ministry of Defence, Germany. He coordinated [STELAR](https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101070122), an EU Horizon project focused on "Artificial Intelligence, Data, and Robotics."  

In 2019, he was awarded the prestigious [Marie Skłodowska-Curie Fellowship](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/) under the EU Horizon 2020 ITN network [PhilHumans](https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/812882/results) where he worked at Philips Research (Netherlands) and the [University of Cagliari](https://aibd.unica.it/people/vivek-kumar) (Italy) and received his doctorate in 2023. Before this, Dr. Kumar worked as a Research Engineer for the project "Search for Hidden Particles" (SHiP) of the European Commission of Nuclear Research (CERN) at NUST-MiSiS.

He has been a reviewer/PC/TPC member and co-chair for several top conferences/workshops, including ACL, EMNLP, IJCAI, ECAI, ECML-PKDD, ACM-SAC, DSAA, ICASSP and several high-impact journals from Elsevier, IEEE, Springer, and MDPI. Dr. Kumar is also a Senior Member of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) and a fellow of the Soft Computing Research Society (SCRS). He is also a member of the International Neural Network Society (INNS) and the International Association of Engineers (IAENG). Dr. Kumar is a seasoned speaker and has delivered several keynote and technical talks.
